<p>District C4 lies between Metric Blvd and North Lamar Blvd., south of West Braker Lane and north of Rutland Drive. It includes area of North Austin Civic Association, Cook Elementary School and Quail Creek Park. NACA Quial Creek, NACA Quial Creek NorthWest and Quial Hollow comprise the C4 District.

Its estimated population is 14,500 based on the US Census Bureau's 2015 American Community Survey. About 60% of its 4800 housing units are renter-occupied. There are many apartment complexes in this district. About 65% of the population is under the age of 40. About 67% are White and 9% are Black. About 20% of the household income is between $50,000 and $75,000 and 60% is less than $50,000. </p>
