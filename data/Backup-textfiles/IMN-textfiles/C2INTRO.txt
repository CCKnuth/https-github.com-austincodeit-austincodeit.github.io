<p>District C2 lies east of Highway 183, south of Highway 290 and north of Highway 71 and the Colorado River and west of TX Toll Highway 130. 
It includes the Walter E. Long Lake and Park area, Colony Park and Bluebonnet Hill Public Golf Course. 
About half the district is in Travis County and the other half part in Bastrop County. 
According to the 2015 American Community Survey conducted by the US Census Bureau, its population is about 17,000 people. 
About 52% of its 4,400 housing units are owner-occupied. 
About 75% of the population is under the age of 40.  
About 63% are White and 25% are Black. 22% of the household income is between $35,000 to $50,000; about 16% is between $25,000 to $35,000, and 16% is less than $ 15,000. </p>
