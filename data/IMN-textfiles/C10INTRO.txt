<p>District C10 is in East Austin, roughly bounded by Airport Blvd. on the southwest, Springdale Rd. on the southeast, and Manor Rd. to the north. In 2015, the US Census Bureau's American Community Survey estimated its population at 8,000. It consists of about 2,750 housing units; roughly half are owner-occupied and other half are renter-occupied.  About 25% of the annual household income is less than $15,000 while 35% of household income is between $35,000 and $75,000. </p>

<p>The area includes portions of three individual planning areas: MLK, MLK-183, and Pecan Springs/Springdale. Givens Park and Morris Williams Golf Course are the two largest park and recreational facilities in the district.
</p>
