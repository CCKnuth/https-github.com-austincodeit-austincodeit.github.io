<p>District C2 lies east of Highway 183, south of Highway 290 and north of Highway 71 and the Colorado river. It includes the Walter E. Long Lake area. About half the district is in Travis County and the other half part in Bastrop County. According to the 2015 American Community Survey conducted by the US Census Bureau, its population is about 17,000 people. About 52% of its 4,411 housing units are owner-occupied. In the population, 63% are White, 25% are Black, and rest are Biracial. 22% of the household income is the highest, between $35,000 to $50,000; about 16% is between $25,000 to $35,000, and 16% is less than $ 15,000. About 73% of the population is less than 39 years old; the age distribution is 24% of the population is less than 9 years old, 18% is between 30 and 39 years old, and remaining 31% is between 10 to 29 years old.  </p>
